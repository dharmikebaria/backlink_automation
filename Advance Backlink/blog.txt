What is Wholesale VoIP?
Wholesale VoIP refers to the bulk transmission of voice calls over IP networks. It is mainly used by:
Telecom operators
VoIP service providers
Call centers
Enterprises with high call volumes
Unlike retail VoIP, wholesale VoIP focuses on high-volume, low-cost voice traffic. It connects carriers, operators, and resellers worldwide using SIP trunks and VoIP gateways.
